Strategic Intent

Tracking what people say on social is essential for brand work, but accessing quality data has real limitations (API access, data completeness, language coverage).

This is a replicated version of the process I built manually at TLGG (Mercedes-Benz) and TD Reply (BMW, L'Oréal). I set it up myself using Python, Apify, and Claude. Limitations are visible in the data — for a more accurate analysis, significantly more effort is required.

Use cases: weekly brand tracking, pre-QBR snapshots, crisis early-warning, and onboarding new brands without an analyst team.

What it produces

  • Multi-source ingestion across Reddit, TikTok, Instagram
  • AI sentiment, topics, and crisis flags per mention (Claude Sonnet 4.5)
  • 2-page PDF executive briefing with top mentions and crisis alerts
  • Looker Studio dashboard with sentiment trends, topic distribution, crisis monitoring

CASE STUDY #2 — IN DEVELOPMENT

AI for Marketplace Operations & Growth

Streamlining merchant analytics, onboarding flows, and marketplace performance

The strategic focus

Based on lessons from launching Houzz Italy and operating within Zalando Marketing Services, scaling a marketplace relies heavily on operational efficiency — balancing merchant acquisition, catalog health, and portfolio performance.

The AI application (in development)

  • Merchant onboarding assistance: AI-driven analysis of onboarding bottlenecks to shorten merchant time-to-first-sale.
  • Portfolio & catalog insights: leveraging automated data extraction to evaluate seller performance, product category gaps, and competitive pricing trends.
  • Operational health dashboards: synthesizing key platform metrics (GMV trends, funnel conversion, merchant retention) into clear operational priorities.
