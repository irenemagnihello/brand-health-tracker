FLAGSHIP CASE STUDY #2

AI-Driven Marketplace Operations & GTM Localization

Automating regulatory compliance, product localization, and catalog operations for international expansion.

The Strategic Intent

  • The Operational Bottleneck: Expanding health, beauty, and consumer product catalogs into international marketplaces requires strict regulatory compliance checks (country-specific dosage limits, mandatory warning texts, and Clean Label ingredient restrictions). Manual cross-border verification severely slows down time-to-market.
  • The AI & No-Code Solution: Built an end-to-end automated GTM localization and compliance engine for Lumina Marketplace connecting a custom Softr front-end app to Make webhooks, Anthropic Claude, and Google Sheets.
  • Proof of Capability: Demonstrates complete operational fluency: connecting a user-friendly Softr GUI to Make automation, evaluating complex regulatory guardrails (e.g., Italian Ministero della Salute, French ANSES, German BfR), and delivering fully translated, compliant product listings and marketing copy at scale.

End-to-End Output Deliverables

  • User-Friendly No-Code Interface: A Softr-based management portal allowing marketplace operations teams to submit SKUs and track real-time GTM readiness.
  • Automated Regulatory & Clean Label Verification: AI-driven compliance checks flagging dosage overages, prohibited additives, and missing statutory legal disclaimers per target market (IT, FR, DE).
  • Marketplace Marketing Copy: Automated generation of localized, high-converting product positioning aligned with regional regulatory limits.
  • Real-Time Operations Sync: Instant pipeline update returning structured execution statuses (Completed, Flagged, Pending) to streamline team reviews.
