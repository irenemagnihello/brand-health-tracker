The Strategic Vision

Why Social Listening Matters: Understanding genuine brand perception in real-time is crucial for high-stakes brand strategy, crisis prevention, and quarterly reviews. However, enterprise listening platforms are expensive, rigid, and often require dedicated data science teams.

The AI-Powered Alternative: Drawing from my experience building multi-channel brand tracking frameworks at TLGG (Mercedes-Benz) and TD Reply (BMW, L'Oréal), I engineered an agile pipeline using Python, Apify, and Claude. This self-built setup proves how a single strategist can leverage modern AI tools to scrape, process, and deliver C-level brand intelligence at a fraction of the traditional cost and time.

End-to-End Output Deliverables

  • Automated Multi-Source Scraping: Ingestion of unstructured public sentiment across TikTok, Instagram, and Reddit.
  • AI Sentiment & Crisis Tagging: Claude Sonnet 4.5 automatically categorizes sentiment, extracts core discussion topics, and flags potential PR risks.
  • Live Executive Dashboard: Looker Studio interface tracking sentiment shifts, volume spikes, and source breakdowns in real time.
  • Automated Briefings: One-click generation of 2-page PDF reports (via ReportLab) formatted directly for C-level leadership.
