Case Study #1

AI-Assisted Brand Perception & Signal Monitoring

Agile social listening and early-warning intelligence for consumer & beauty brands.

Why social listening matters

Tracking what people say about a brand on social media is essential — it surfaces perception shifts, complaint patterns, and product feedback weeks before any survey would catch them. But getting reliable cross-platform data at scale is hard. Most platforms restrict scraping, sentiment APIs are costly, and real-time monitoring tools charge premium prices.

This is a replicated version of the process I used to run manually at TLGG (Mercedes) and TD Reply (BMW, L'Oréal). I set it up myself using Python, Apify, and Claude. Limitations are visible in the data — for a more accurate analysis, significantly more effort is required.

The AI-Augmented Setup

I designed an agile intelligence workflow using Python scrapers and LLMs (Claude) to ingest raw, unstructured user content across TikTok, Reddit, and Instagram.

  • Multi-source ingestion: scrapes mentions and discussions across high-engagement social channels.
  • LLM data processing: evaluates sentiment, categorizes main conversation topics, and flags operational/PR risk signals per mention.
  • Executive summary: feeds automated data into a Looker Studio dashboard and generates a structured 2-page briefing snapshot for fast team review.

Strategic scope & reality check

"This setup does not replace deep, multi-million-euro market research frameworks like the ones I managed for clients like BMW or ELC. Instead, it serves as a fast, flexible early-stage monitor — giving teams an immediate pulse on market perception and early signals at a fraction of the traditional turnaround time."
