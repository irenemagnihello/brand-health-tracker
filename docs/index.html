What it produces

  • Scrapes mentions across Reddit, TikTok, and Instagram for 3 beauty brands
  • AI sentiment, topics, and crisis flags per mention (Claude Sonnet 4.5)
  • Weekly 2-page PDF executive briefing with top mentions and crisis alerts
  • Live dashboard with sentiment trends, topic distribution, crisis monitoring

Why I built this

Brand monitoring work I used to build manually at TLGG and TD Reply (Mercedes, BMW, L'Oréal) — same output, faster turnaround, fully independent.

Use cases: weekly brand tracking, pre-QBR snapshots, crisis early-warning, and onboarding new brands without an analyst team.

Note on social listening: tracking what people say on social is important for brand work, but accessing quality data has real limitations (API access, data completeness, language coverage). This is a replicated version of the process I built manually before — limitations are visible. For more accurate analysis, deeper setup and paid APIs are required.
